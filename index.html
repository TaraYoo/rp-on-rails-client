A campaign trackers for lazy GMs who have trouble keeping up with all their locations and plots

map

List out your locations

Create, edit, delete, and track your campaign locations in card format. Additionally, you can pull a random risk or reward to serve as a quick encounter while you think of other plots when your players when they decide to travel elsewhere

group

Involve your players

Ask your players to come up with rewards, such as an overpowered item, or a diplomatic triumph. Make them give you a proportionate risk, such as a lich queen or betrayals. Add them to a black box to draw from at random.

school

Designed for lazy GMs

The app is designed for GMs who want to have minimal plot points per location, while involving players in building the game. Please see Zee Bashew's How I DM video for full details



Made by Tara Yoo with Materialize, Javascript, and Ruby on Rails
